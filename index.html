délicieux produits de boulangerie

Bread may be the most basic of all foods, but, in France choosing bread is also an institution. We  the French care more about bread and the types of bread available than any other nation. When you visit France look for an opportunity at breakfast or even better, on a picnic where you may have an excuse to buy three or four different breads of your own choosing. On that picnic take along a couple of cheeses, a pate and an enjoyable bottle of wine; then French bread will make its preeminence clear. Bon appetite!

Histoire de Cafe

Cafés Verlet  serve as a center of social and culinary life in Paris. They have been around since the 17th century in one form or another, the oldest one still in operation is "Café Procope" at 13 rue de l'Ancienne Comédie, since 1686.
